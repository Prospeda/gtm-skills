# GTM Sequences

Complete multi-touch outreach cadences that get responses.

## Sequence Principles

1. **Multi-channel** - Email alone gets ignored; layer in LinkedIn and phone
2. **Value-first** - Every touch should provide value, not just "checking in"
3. **Persistence with grace** - Follow up without being annoying
4. **Personalization** - Generic = ignored; specific = responses
5. **Clear CTAs** - One ask per message

## 14-Day Enterprise Sequence

For VP+ at companies 500+ employees.

```
Day 1  [Email]    First touch - personalized, trigger-based
Day 2  [LinkedIn] Connection request - reference email
Day 3  [Phone]    Call + voicemail if no answer
Day 5  [Email]    Follow-up - new angle/value add
Day 7  [LinkedIn] Engage with their content
Day 8  [Phone]    Second call attempt
Day 10 [Email]    Case study or relevant insight
Day 12 [LinkedIn] InMail or DM if connected
Day 14 [Email]    Breakup email
```

### Day 1: Email - First Touch
```
Subject: {Trigger}-based subject

Hi {Name},

{Personalization - 1 sentence about them/company}

{Pain point question or insight - 1-2 sentences}

We've helped {similar company} {specific result}.

Worth a 15-minute chat to see if there's a fit?

{Signature}
```

### Day 2: LinkedIn Connection
```
Hi {Name}, sent you a note yesterday about {topic}.
Would love to connect here too.
```

### Day 3: Phone + Voicemail
```
[If voicemail]
"Hi {Name}, it's {Your name} following up on my email
about {topic}. Give me a call at {number} if it's relevant.
If not, no worries."
```

### Day 5: Email - Follow-Up
```
Subject: Re: {Original subject}

Hi {Name},

Quick follow-up. Wanted to share {insight/stat/article}
that's relevant to {their situation}.

{1 sentence connecting it to them}

Still open to a quick chat?

{Signature}
```

### Day 10: Email - Case Study
```
Subject: How {Similar Company} solved {problem}

Hi {Name},

Thought you'd find this relevant - {Similar Company}
was dealing with {same pain} and {achieved result}.

Here's the quick version: {2-3 bullet summary}

Would their approach work at {Company}?

{Signature}

P.S. Happy to share the full case study if useful.
```

### Day 14: Email - Breakup
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi {Name},

I've reached out a few times without hearing back.

I'm going to assume the timing isn't right and stop
filling your inbox.

If things change, I'm here. Just reply to this email.

All the best with {relevant wish},
{Signature}
```

## 10-Day Mid-Market Sequence

For Directors/VPs at 100-500 employee companies.

```
Day 1  [Email]    First touch
Day 2  [LinkedIn] Connection request
Day 4  [Email]    Follow-up + value add
Day 6  [Phone]    Call attempt
Day 7  [LinkedIn] Content engagement
Day 10 [Email]    Breakup
```

## 7-Day SMB Sequence

For smaller companies - move faster, less touches.

```
Day 1  [Email]    First touch - direct, short
Day 3  [Email]    Follow-up - different angle
Day 5  [Phone]    Call + voicemail
Day 7  [Email]    Breakup
```

## Re-Engagement Sequence

For leads that went dark after initial interest.

```
Day 1  [Email]    "Things change" check-in
Day 4  [LinkedIn] Share relevant content
Day 7  [Email]    New trigger or news about them
Day 10 [Phone]    Direct call
Day 14 [Email]    Final breakup
```

### Day 1: Re-Engagement Email
```
Subject: {Company} + {Your Company} - revisiting

Hi {Name},

We spoke back in {month} about {topic}.
At the time, {reason it didn't move forward}.

Wanted to check if anything's changed on your end.

{New value prop or trigger since then}

Worth revisiting?

{Signature}
```

## Event Follow-Up Sequence

After meeting at conference/webinar.

```
Day 1  [Email]    "Great meeting you" + specific reference
Day 2  [LinkedIn] Connection request + event reference
Day 4  [Email]    Share promised resource/follow-up
Day 7  [Email]    Suggest next step
Day 10 [Email]    Breakup if no response
```

### Day 1: Post-Event Email
```
Subject: Great meeting you at {Event}

Hi {Name},

Really enjoyed our conversation at {Event} about {topic}.

Your point about {specific thing they said} stuck with me.

As promised, here's {resource you mentioned}.

Would love to continue the conversation - how's {day} for a quick call?

{Signature}
```

## Inbound Follow-Up Sequence

When they downloaded content or visited pricing.

```
Hour 1   [Email]    Immediate - resource + soft CTA
Day 1    [Phone]    Call while they're engaged
Day 2    [Email]    Follow-up + related resource
Day 4    [Phone]    Second call attempt
Day 5    [Email]    Direct ask for meeting
Day 7    [LinkedIn] Connection + message
Day 10   [Email]    Breakup
```

### Hour 1: Inbound Response
```
Subject: {Resource name} + a question

Hi {Name},

Thanks for downloading {resource}.

Quick question: what prompted your interest in {topic}?

Asking because we help {persona}s like you with {value prop},
and I want to make sure I'm sending relevant info.

Also happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier.

{Signature}
```

## Sequence A/B Tests

Test these variables:

| Variable | Option A | Option B |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Subject line | Question | Statement |
| Email length | Short (50 words) | Medium (100 words) |
| CTA | Time-specific | Open-ended |
| Personalization | Company-level | Person-level |
| Touch count | 5 touches | 8 touches |
| Channel mix | Email-heavy | Multi-channel |

## Sequence Metrics

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Open rate | 40% | 60%+ |
| Reply rate | 5% | 10%+ |
| Meeting rate | 2% | 5%+ |
| Positive reply % | 30% of replies | 50%+ |
